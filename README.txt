Instructions

*The UI sizes, designs, and placements are all temporary*

Use the arrow keys to move around.
Press E when you move over a companion to collect it.
Companion costs are shown next to them.
The strawberry shoots 3 seeds towards the nearest enemy.
The pineapple shoots one projectile (that will explode upon impact soon).
Each ant has 3 hp and drops one coin upon death. Move over it to collect it.
Win the level by hitting the mouse with enough projectiles.

Esc to pause
R to resume
Esc to close settings
(Settings doesn't do anything)
