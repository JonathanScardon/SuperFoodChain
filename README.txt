Instructions

Use the arrow keys to move around.
You cannot move backwards.
Move over a companion to collect it.
Companion costs are shown next to them.
The strawberry shoots 3 seeds towards the nearest enemy (long range).
The durian shoots a ring of projectiles (short range).
The blue raspberry increases the speed of your chain.
Each minion drops one coin upon death. Move over it to collect it.
Win the level by hitting the boss with enough projectiles.

Esc to pause
Esc to close settings
(Settings doesn't do anything)
